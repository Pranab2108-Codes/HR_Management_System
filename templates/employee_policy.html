Official Company Policy

Please read and understand the rules regarding attendance, leaves, and daily workflows below.

⏱️ Employee Policy

Office Timings

  • Shift: 9:15 AM – 6:30 PM
  • Grace Period: Up to 9:30 AM
  • Note: Reporting even 1 minute beyond 9:30 AM will not be considered under the grace period.

Attendance

  • All employees are expected to report to the office within the prescribed office timings.
  • Repeated late reporting may lead to disciplinary action.
  • Employees must inform their Reporting Manager and HR in case of delay or absence.

🏢 HR Policy

General HR Guidelines

  • All employees must comply with the company rules, regulations, and code of conduct.
  • Employees are required to maintain professional behavior in the workplace.
  • HR reserves the right to review and update policies as required.

👋 On-boarding Policy

Joining Formalities

  • Employees must submit all required documents during joining.
  • The probation period shall be 3 months unless otherwise specified.
  • During the on-boarding process, employees will be informed about company policies, reporting structure, and responsibilities.

🚪 Termination Policy

Notice Period

  • During Probation: 15 days notice.
  • After Confirmation: 30 days notice.
  • Immediate separation may require notice pay as per company policy.

🌴 Leave Policy

Probationary Employees

  • Employees may avail up to 1 day of sick/emergency leave per month during probation.
  • Any additional leave will be treated as Leave Without Pay.

Confirmed Employees

  • Employees earn 1 day of leave for every 20 days worked.
  • Employees are entitled to up to 12 days of Sick Leave per year.
  • All leave must be approved by the Reporting Manager and HR before availing.
